Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

We're open daily from 9 AM to 7 PM during (April-October), and we are open 7 days a week. Hours may vary during holidays and special events. Check our calendar for specific dates.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! Special rates are available for groups of 15 or more. Contact Jaura's Theme Park Group Sales team for details.

What's your rain policy?

The park remains open during light rain. Some rides may close temporarily during severe weather.

Are outside food and drinks allowed?

Small snacks and water bottles are permitted. No large coolers or alcoholic beverages are allowed at the jaura's theme park.
