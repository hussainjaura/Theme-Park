Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about The Jaura's Theme Park

What are your operating hours?

We're open daily from 9 AM to 10 PM during peak season (June-August), and 10 AM to 8 PM during off-peak season. Hours may vary during holidays and special events. Check our calendar for specific dates.

What's your ticket pricing structure?

Adult tickets (13+): £45
Child tickets (3-12): £35
Senior tickets (65+): £35
Under 3: Free
Special discounts available for group bookings and season passes.

What are the height and age restrictions for rides?

Height requirements vary by ride, ranging from 90cm to 140cm. Each ride has clear signage indicating minimum height requirements. Some attractions may also have maximum height restrictions for safety reasons.

Do you offer food alternatives for dietary restrictions?

Yes, our restaurants offer vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergen-free options. Please inform our staff about any dietary requirements, and they'll be happy to assist you.

What's your bad weather policy?

The park remains open during light rain, but some rides may close temporarily during severe weather conditions. If the park closes due to extreme weather, guests will receive compensation options.

Are there storage lockers available?

Yes, lockers are available throughout the park. Small lockers: £5/day, Large lockers: £8/day. Digital payment options available.

Do you offer disability access?

Yes, we provide comprehensive accessibility services including wheelchair rentals, accessible queuing areas, and companion passes. Please visit our Guest Services desk for assistance.

Can I bring my pet to the park?

Only service animals are permitted in the park. We do not allow personal pets for safety reasons. A pet daycare facility is available nearby.

Do you offer photography services?

Yes, professional photographers are stationed throughout the park. Photo packages start at £25. Digital downloads and printed options available.

What's included in the VIP experience?

VIP packages include priority queue access, exclusive lounge access, complimentary refreshments, personal guide, and special viewing areas for shows. Contact Guest Services for pricing.
